# Privacy Policy for Yumie

**Last Updated:** August 5, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Yumie ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you use Yumie:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and profile photo (if provided)
- **Health and Fitness Data**: 
  - Age, height, weight, target weight
  - Activity level and blood type
  - Medical conditions (e.g., diabetes status)
  - Daily calorie goals and macronutrient targets (protein, carbs, fat)
  - Water intake preferences and tracking
- **Meal and Nutrition Data**:
  - Food items consumed and their nutritional information
  - Meal photos and AI-analyzed food recognition data
  - Custom meals and recipes
  - Dietary preferences and restrictions
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction data

### 2.2 Device and Technical Information
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Camera access for food scanning and photo capture
- Location data (if you enable location-based features)
- App performance data and crash reports
- Network information and connection status

### 2.3 Payment Information
- Subscription purchase history and payment details
- Billing information (processed securely through app stores)
- Subscription status and renewal dates

### 3.3 Communication
- Send important app updates and notifications
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

### 3.4 Legal and Security
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
